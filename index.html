Cycurion, Inc. Cybersecurity Solutions

Strategically positioned in the cybersecurity sector through acquisitions and innovation

Company Evolution

Initially incorporated as KAE Holdings, Inc. in Delaware (October 2017), Cycurion focuses on acquiring and holding entities in the cybersecurity sector. Renamed from Cyber Secure Solutions, Inc. in 2021, the company has expanded through strategic acquisitions including Axxum Technologies LLC, Cloudburst Security LLC, and Cycurion Innovation, Inc.

Strategic Acquisitions

The acquisition of technology assets from Sabres Security (an Israeli cybersecurity leader) enhances Cycurion's service offerings, incorporating cutting-edge SaaS platforms like Multi-Dimensional Protection (MDP), Web Application Firewall (WAF), and Bot Mitigation.

Business Operations

Cycurion operates through three wholly-owned subsidiaries—Axxum Technologies, Cloudburst Security, and Cycurion Innovation—delivering tailored cybersecurity solutions across federal and commercial sectors.

Government & Commercial Reach

Through prime and subcontractor relationships, notably with SLG Innovation, Cycurion has secured significant state and local government contracts, further cementing its position in the cybersecurity industry.
